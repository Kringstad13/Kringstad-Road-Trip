Frequently Asked Questions

Do family trips get a different dashboard than business trips?

No! Everyone gets the same professional dashboard interface with the same tabs and features. What changes is the AI-generated content:

  • Family trips ‚Üí kid-friendly hotels, playgrounds, snack lists
  • Business trips ‚Üí conference hotels, client dinners, expense tracking
  • Romantic trips ‚Üí fine dining, scenic stops, couple activities

Same tool. Different suggestions. That's the power of AI customization.

How is this different from Google Maps?

Google Maps shows you routes. RouteBuilder AI creates a complete trip planning experience with hotels, restaurants, budget tracking, packing lists, and entertainment‚Äîall in one beautiful dashboard.

Absolutely! Just tell us what you want to change: "Add a stop in Denver" or "Increase hotel budget to $600" and we'll regenerate your dashboard instantly.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes! Your dashboard works perfectly on phones, tablets, and computers. It even works offline once downloaded.

Can I use this for my travel business?

Yes! Our Pro plan is designed specifically for travel agents, tour operators, and corporate travel coordinators. You can even white-label it with Enterprise.

How does photo sharing work?

When you share your dashboard link with family or friends, everyone can view AND upload photos in real-time. As soon as someone uploads a photo from the Grand Canyon, everyone with the link sees it instantly. No more "did you get my text with the pics?"

Nope! Unlimited modifications are included. Change the route, adjust your budget, add stops, swap hotels‚Äîas many times as you want. We know plans change, so we make it easy.
